DesktopRenamer

Rename Your Experiences.

Overview

Take back control of your macOS spaces.

DesktopRenamer is a macOS menubar app that lets you give custom names to your desktop spaces — those virtual desktops you create in Mission Control.
Features

Everything you need to own your workflow.

01 / Rename

Rename at Menu Bar

Give your desktop spaces custom names directly from the menu bar. Click, type, done.

02 / Labels

Crystal Clear Labels

Aesthetic name labels in Mission Control. A discreet active label confirms your space after switching.

03 / Navigate

Switch & Move

Click a space to jump to it. Hold ⌥ to teleport the active window — no dragging.

04 / Speed

Faster Switching

Bypass macOS animation lag with trackpad overrides or keyboard hotkeys for instant switching.

Raycast

Manage and switch spaces from Raycast, your favorite launcher.

Install Raycast Extension
API & AppleScript

Full AppleScript and API for automating space management from your own scripts and tools.

Widget

See your current space and tap to switch — directly from the Notification Center or Desktop widget.

Install

Get started in seconds.

macOS 13.0+. No SIP disable. Drag to Applications, done.

Download
or brew install --cask gitmichaelqiu/tap/desktoprenamer

Not signed with a paid Apple Developer account — go to System Settings → Privacy & Security if macOS blocks the app. Permissions are guided on first launch.
